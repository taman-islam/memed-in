A Chrome extension that turns your social media feeds into a meme by summarizing posts and profiles into their honest, spicy subtext.

## Vision

Turn LinkedIn feeds and Facebook profiles into a meme by summarizing them into their true intent. "If this person were honest, what are they really saying?"

## Features

- **LinkedIn:** Summarize individual LinkedIn posts into their honest social-intent subtext with one click.
- **Facebook:** Instantly generate a spicy, meme-worthy summary of any Facebook user's profile.
- **Configurable AI Prompts:** Fully customize the AI instructions and tone for both platforms from the extension options page.
- **Summarize Again:** Easily bypass caching and regenerate summaries if you want a fresh take.

## Quick Start

1. **Get an API Key:** Get a free Gemini API key from [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/).
2. **Install:** Go to `chrome://extensions/`, enable **Developer mode**, click **Load unpacked**, and select this folder.
3. **Configure:** The options page will open automatically. Paste your API key, customize your specific AI meme prompts (optional), and click **Save**.

## How to Use

### On LinkedIn

1. Navigate to your [LinkedIn Feed](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/).
2. You will see a "Summarize" button next to the three-dot menu on valid posts.
3. Click the button to inject a custom AI meme summary right above the post text.

### On Facebook

1. Navigate to any user's profile on Facebook.
2. A "Summarize Profile" button will appear at the top of their page.
3. Click it to scrape their profile and receive a spicy, meme-style summary about who they are.
